Racket Services

Keep your racket ready.

Desert Palm Racquet Club offers racket support for players who want reliable stringing, fresh grips, and straightforward setup advice.

The focus is simple: help the racket feel right for the player, their level, and the way they play.

01

Restringing

Fresh strings matched to your game, level, preferred feel, and playing style.

02

Grips

Replacement grips and overgrips for comfort, control, hygiene, and a better feel in the hand.

03

Setup Advice

Practical guidance on strings, tension, racket choice, and small setup adjustments.

Player Support

Do not string what is famous.

String what makes you a better player. The right setup should support your game, not just follow the most popular choice.
